The Colusa Bio Innovation Center is the new home of the Northern California bio-materials industry. Embedded in the powerful Colusa agricultural & transportation region, this is the facility for emerging biotech start-ups transitioning from R&D into full scale production.

CBIC start-ups and tenants are supported with unique Access to Capital, Direct Supply Chain Assistance, Transportation Solutions, and rare Industry or Institution Partnerships. Your research and development comes alive here!

The facility’s wet lab is being outfitted to support full materials characterization and late stage product development. Additionally, CBIC’s ‘Commercialization Zone’ will feature low-rate initial production equipment specifically for startups with hi-line beta products needing to scale up to consistent quality and fulfill early sales.

Bio-materials innovations with a chemical, engineered process, ag-waste stream diversion, bio-fuels development, alternative building materials, fermentation, bio-composites/plastics and more have a home at the Colusa Bio Innovation Center. The future of Northern California biomaterials is here!
