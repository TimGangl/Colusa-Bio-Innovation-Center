Have a great bio-materials product?

Starting to make sales, but don’t have the right facility to jump into larger production?

Need money and help to get your start-up to the next level?

Contact Colusa Bio Innovation Center:

cbic@cityofcolusa.com

CBIC is a program run by the City of Colusa engaging bio-materials entrepreneurs’ products and helping them jump into commercialization. The City-owned facility is available for start-ups of all sizes and seeks to create an industrial ecology with multiple stakeholders while tapping into the agricultural strength of the region.

  • low-rate production level equipment available to rent by the hour for entrepreneurs
  • An adjacent wet lab
  • Access to Various Types of Capital (Grants, Loans, Investor Network)
  • Direct Supply Chain Assistance
  • Connection to Customers
  • Featured at CBIC Events

Use The Facility:

For more information on using the facility, contact: cbic@cityofcolusa.com

LEVEL 1: Rent an Office and lease hours on the wet lab or using the commercialization equipment. This is a flat monthly fee and gives you an office plus access to the conference rooms, and other office amenities. A guaranteed number of hours are available in the Wet-Lab and for Commercialization Zone equipment. Additionally, members are given a designated amount of warehouse storage for raw materials or packaging. All spaces are shared at this level.

LEVEL 2: This level gives you additional raw space up to 40,000 square feet to create your own pilot-processing and still use the wet lab and commercialization equipment. If you need more space to store or pre-process raw materials, or perhaps you have a specialty piece of equipment, then this is the level for you. Level 2 tenants also get an office space and office amenities.

LEVEL 3: Tenants at level 3 are doing more robust sales and are likely utilizing 40,000-80,000 square feet of their own dedicated space. Level 3 tenants have access to the wet lab and the commercialization zone. However, the expectation is that they don’t need the equipment in the commercialization zone for more than product testing or new prototypes. Level 3 tenants get more than one office depending on need and all office amenities.

OFFICE AMENITIES: Desks, chairs, conference tables, break room supplies, stocked restrooms, front desk personnel on select days, facility scheduling assistance, cleaning services, free WiFi, shared copier.

QUARTERLY EVENTS: The “Quarterly Cocktails in Colusa” event is structured as a friendly meet & greet event with a guest speaker and investors looking for new projects. Companies can decide to showcase their products, do nominal pitching and expand their network.
